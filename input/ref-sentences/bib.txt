Yeezuu yaabɔɔ Yeezuu yaabɔɔ wɔɔ ba n nɩ: Yeezuu ɩ yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, cir *Davɩd Nyɩ m, Abraham do gʊaa m sɔ.
Abraham n Izakkɩ yɩ, Izakkɩ n Zakɔɔb yɩ, Zakɔɔb n Ziida kan a dannɔ kɩ yɩ, Ziida n Farɛɛs kan Zaaraa kɩ yɩ, ŋ da m Tamaar m, Farɛɛs n Ɛsrɔm yɩ, Ɛsrɔm n Aram yɩ, Aram n Aminadaab yɩ, Aminadaab n Naasɔɔn yɩ, Naasɔɔn n Salmɔɔn yɩ, Salmɔɔn n Boozɩ yɩ, a da m Rahab m, Boozɩ n Zobɛɛd yɩ, a da m Rutɩ m, Zobɛɛd n Yesee yɩ, Yesee n cir Davɩd yɩ, Davɩd n Salomɔɔn yɩ, a da nyɩnta Iirii lʊ, Salomɔɔn n Roboam yɩ, Roboam n Abɩya yɩ, Abɩya n Azaa yɩ, Azaa n Zozafat yɩ, Zozafat n Zooram yɩ, Zooram n Ozɩyas yɩ, Ozɩyas n Zowatan yɩ, Zowatan n Akaazɩ yɩ, Akaazɩ n Ezekɩyas yɩ, Ezekɩyas n Manase yɩ, Manase n Amɔɔn yɩ, Amɔɔn n Zozɩyas yɩ, Zozɩyas n Zekonɩyas kan a dannɔ kɩ yɩ.
A n a ga kʊ ʊ kan *Babɩlɔnnɩ lɔmbaa tarɛ dɔmɩm bɩ kɩ.
Babɩlɔnnɩ lɔmbaa bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Zekonɩyas Salatɩyɛl yɩ, Salatɩyɛl n Zorobabɛl yɩ, Zorobabɛl n Abiyud yɩ, Abiyud n Eliyakim yɩ, Eliyakim n Azɔɔr yɩ, Azɔɔr n Sadɔk yɩ, Sadɔk n Akim yɩ, Akim n Eliyud yɩ, Eliyud n Eleyazaar yɩ, Eleyazaar n Matan yɩ, Matan n Zakɔɔb yɩ, Zakɔɔb n Maarii zim Zozɛf yɩ, Maarii bɩ do ɩ Yeezuu yɩ n n'a birm Krista.
Yaabɔɔ wɔɔ haay lɛ a zɛ kʊ ra bueeresi-si.
A n a sa Abraham ma an ta m Davɩd ra bueeresi, an a sa Davɩd ma an ta m Babɩlɔnnɩ lɔmbaa ma bueeresi, an a sa Babɩlɔnnɩ lɔmbaa ma an ta m Krista ra bueeresi.
Yeezuu yɩrɛ Yeezuu yɩrɛ bɩ zaa ba n nɩ: A da Maarii bɩ, nyɩnta a ʊ Zozɛf jaan, bɩ, an lɛɛ ka kʊ Maarii n a ba Zozɛf lʊ bɩ, *Sɛnt-Ɛspri a ka Maarii n jɩsɩ sa.
A jaan Zozɛf bɩ kʊ gʊaa mɩŋŋa m bɩ, a n a ŋʊa k'a a da nyannɩ ʊ y.
A n a hɔɔn da k'a wɔ saa kʊ gɔsɩ b'a dɔ y. K'a n'a hɔɔn darɛ ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, Zuuba Malɛɛka bʊr zi nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Zozɛf, Davɩd do gʊaa, ɩ bɩ nyi ba k'ɩ Maarii sa ɩ ba ɩ lʊ y, bala, Sɛnt-Ɛspri paŋŋa y'a ka, an nɩ jɩsɩ bɩ m, a nyɩ yɩm yar, k'ɩ tɔ ka Yeezuu, bala, a m n'a do bɩ bʊmbɔrɛ a mimbʊnyaarɔ nɔ ʊ.» Naa do woo… naa, ba ʊ kʊ meerbaa kʊ Woso a ba kan a lɛsinnɩsorazaa lɛ kɩ bɩ n kʊ yɩ.
A ba n nɩ: nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ k'a n yar dɔ bɩ jɩsɩ sim, an nyɩ yɩ yar, n y'a tɔ karɛ Emanʊwɛl, a jɩ lɛ taa k'a hɩ, a ʊ: Woso ɩ kan wɔɔ kɩ.
Zozɛf k'a bɩ gu hinceem ma bɩ, a hɔ kʊ Zuuba malɛɛka b'a hɩ m bɩ ba: A Maarii gaa, an a ba a lʊ, bɩ, a n a dɔ lʊ y, an ta an aa dɔmɩm k'a nyɩyar bɩ yɩ ʊ bɩ ku, an a tɔ ka Yeezuu.
Hɔdonno bʊr k'a bɩ dɩga Yeezuu ma Yeezuu bɩ, yɩ a ʊ Betelehɛm ʊ Zidee kʊ ʊ, cir Hɩrɔɔdɩ dɔmɩm ʊ, bɩ hɔdonno a to dɔjɩr ʊ, ŋnɩ bʊr Zerizalɛm ʊ, ŋnɩ laaka da: «Zɩɩfʊrɔ cir k'a yɩ b'ɩ ka?
Wɔɔ monyaa yɩ dɔjɩr ʊ bɩ y'a ka, wɔɔ n bʊr k'ʊ b'a da ʊ.» Hɩrɔɔdɩ k'a naa ma bɩ, a da bɔ ʊ kan Zerizalɛm zannɔ kɩ haay.
A n wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ wɔɔ so kʊ ma kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ, an laaka da ŋ han kʊ niŋŋə kʊ Krista bɩ n nɩ yɩŋ ʊ bɩ ma.
Brɔɔ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: Betelehɛm ʊ Zidee kʊ ʊ, bala, hɔ kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa b'a hɩ bɩ n naa do.
B'ɩbɩɩ Betelehɛm, Zidee kʊ, ɩbɩɩ bɛɛ m poore Zidee kʊrɔ gʊtɔɔ wɔɔ barla y. Bala, cir bɔrɛ ɩbɩɩ bireyaa ʊ, an ka Israyɛl mɔɔ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ lɛɛ.
A m bɩ, Hɩrɔɔdɩ n hɔdonno wɔɔ bir sɛɛrɛ, an laaka da ŋ han dɔmɩm kʊ monyaa bɩ bɔ ʊ bɩ ma.
A n ŋ nyɔɔ Betelehɛm ʊ, an n'a hɩrɛ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a ta a laaka da nyɩ bɩ mim ma mɩŋŋa, k'a nyɩ bɩ yɩ, k'a a wusigə a b'a hɩ mɔɔ m, kʊ m ta m aa da ʊ sɔ.» Cir bɩ k'a naa hɩ bɩ, ŋ y'a hʊr, ta kʊ ŋ nawʊm bɩ, monyaa kʊ ŋ lɛɛ ka ŋn'a yɩ dɔjɩr b'ɩ tarɛ ŋ lɛɛ, an ta an aa jɩm lɛɛ kʊ nyɩ bɩ n ta ʊ bɩ burə ʊ. Monyaa bɩ kʊ ŋ y'a yɩ bɩ, ŋ heer yɩ nyɩnta gʊta.
Ŋ gasʊ cɛ b'ʊ, ŋn'aa nyɩ bɩ yɩ kan a da Maarii kɩ, ŋnɩ kukurə zɛ a taa, ŋnɩ yaa da m, bɩ jɛ ʊ, ŋn'a mɔdrɔ wɔɔ hʊr, ŋnɩ gan wʊ ma: Sarma m kan tidəər nyisi kɩ, *mɩɩr kɩ.
Bɩ Woso a hɩ ŋ nɩ nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ, a ʊ: ŋ b'a ya, ŋnɩ bɔ Hɩrɔɔdɩ zi y. Naa minto bɩ, brɔɔ zaa vanta sa ŋnɩ doo a kʊ ʊ. Eziptɩ kʊ tarɛ A hʊr kʊ ŋ y'a ʊ bɩ, Zuuba malɛɛka a hɩnka Zozɛf m nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Wuti ɩ nyɩ bɩ gaa kan a da bɩ kɩ, ɩ ɩ si, ɩ ta Eziptɩ ʊ, nyɩnta b'ʊ k'an ta an aa dɔmɩm kʊ mɔɔ n nɩ m yarɛ m meer ba kan ɩbɩɩ kɩ bɩ ku, bala, Hɩrɔɔdɩ y'a kam nyɩ bɩ ma, k'a zɛ.» Zozɛf n wuti, an nyɩ bɩ kan a da bɩ kɩ gaa gunuu ʊ, an a hʊr an ta Eziptɩ ʊ.
A gɔɔta b'ʊ an ta, an Hɩrɔɔdɩ garɛ ku, kʊ Woso meerbaa k'a ba kan a lɛsinnɩsorazaa lɛ kɩ bɩ n kʊ yɩ.
Mɔɔ m Nyɩ bɩ bir, k'an bɔ Eziptɩ ʊ. Betelehɛm nyɩnɔ poorero zɩnzɛrɛ Hɩrɔɔdɩ k'a b'a dɔ a ʊ, hɔdonno wɔɔ a yamba a m bɩ, a wuti nɔmaa gandaara m. A n gʊɔɔ nyɔɔ Betelehɛm ʊ kan kʊrɔ vantɔɔ k'a a so ŋ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ ʊ, kʊ ŋ n'aa nyɩnɔ poorero yaarɔ kʊ ŋ dɔɔraa hɩɩya gɛɛ ŋ n a ku rɔ wɔɔ zɩnzɛ.
Amba hɔdonno wɔɔ a hɩ m, nyɩnta k'a ʊ, an laaka da k'a nyɩ bɩ yɩrɛ dɔmɩm dɔ bɩ ma.
Naa ma bɩ, hɔ kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa Zeremii a hɩ bɩ kʊ yɩ, a ʊ: N zeedarɛ ma Ramaa ʊ, nyikəre kan heerzarlɛ gʊta kɩ: Rasɛl nɩ nyi karɛ a nyɩnɔ minto, a lɛ baa kʊ n n'a wɔ zɩnzɛ a nɩ y, nyɩnɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ gɩnga bɩ tɔ ma.
Zozɛf bɔ Eziptɩ ʊ Hɩrɔɔdɩ garɛ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Zuuba malɛɛka a hɩnka Zozɛf m nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ Eziptɩ ʊ.
A n meer ba kan kɩ, a ʊ: «Wuti, ɩ nyɩ bɩ kan a da bɩ kɩ gaa ɩ ɩ wusigə, ɩ ta Israyɛl tara ra.
Bala, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'a kam ma k'a nyɩ bɩ meeyaa bɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ gɩnga.» Zozɛf n wuti, an nyɩ bɩ kan a da bɩ kɩ gaa, an ta Israyɛl ʊ. Bɩ, Zozɛf k'a a ma, n ʊ, Arkelayus nɩ cirbəə m Zidee ʊ a zɩ Hɩrɔɔdɩ bɩncɛ ʊ bɩ, nyibəə a nyasʊ k'an ta b'ʊ bɩ kɩ, bɩ Woso meer ba kan kɩ nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ, an ta Galilee kʊ ʊ.
A n aa nyɩnta kʊ kʊ n n'a hɩm ma Nazarɛtɩ ʊ b'ʊ, k'an bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ hɔ kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ wɔɔ a hɩ bɩ n kʊ yɩ, ŋ ʊ: N y'a birm Nazarɛtɩ gʊaa.
Zaan-Batiisi ɩ Woso lemim pa dam Dɔmɩnnɔ wɔɔ duro ʊ bɩ, Zaan-Batiisi bʊr Zidee poo nɔ ʊ, an nɩ Woso lemim pa dam.
A n n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «K'a a bɔ a taam, k'a sugur yɛ, bala, Woso cirbəə b'a zɔ.» A mɩŋŋa wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa Ezayii a hɩ ma, a ʊ: Gʊaa leer nɩ bɔm poo nɔ ʊ: k'a Zuuba zaa banka, k'a zaa fɔɔrɛrɔ wɔɔ bɩmbanka kʊ ŋ nɩ yɩ tɩrga.
Zaan bɩ, yoonde ka huu a a du a ma, an kʊmbar sa an a so a ʊ, bɩsɩnɔ kan zɔɔn kɩ n a hɔbɩrɛ.
A m naa, Zerizalɛm, Zidee kʊ bɩ haay, kɔ k'a tɔ m Zʊrdɛn kʊrɔ wɔɔ haay nɩ tam zi, ŋ n'a mimbʊnyaarɔ hɩrɛ, Zaan nɩ batɛm karɛ ŋ ʊ Zʊrdɛn kɔ ʊ. K'a farizɛɛnrɔ kan sadisɛɛnrɔ kɩ yɩ ŋn'ɩ zɛm gʊta batɛm sire bɩncɛ ʊ bɩ, a a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Mɩnnɔ kʊsɩrɔ do, nka y'a hɩ awɔɔ m, a ʊ, a a si Woso nɔmaa gʊta k'a nɩ zɛm bɩ lɛɛ?
K'a zibəə k'a n'a hɩnkarɛ a ʊ, awɔɔ heerlʊrlɛ yɩ bɩ ba.
B'a b'a hɔɔn da a ʊ, awɔɔ zɩ m Abraham nɩ y. Bala, mɔɔ y'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, Woso a da ma an jaarɔ nɔɔn duro wusigə an ŋ ba Abraham nyɩnɔ.
Bɩ dɩɩtaa naa, n sɩga banka k'an gɔrɔ zɩnzɛ ra ŋ məsi ma, gɔ niŋŋə k'a bɩr nyɩ mɩŋŋa darɛ y, n y'a zɛm la, n n a da sɛ ʊ.» Hi batɛm kan sɛ batɛm kɩ «Mɔɔ ɩ batɛm karɛ awɔɔ ʊ hi ʊ a heer k'awɔɔ lʊr bɩ minto.
Bɩ, an nɩ zɛm mɔɔ jɛ ʊ bɩ paŋŋa lɛ da mɔɔ ra, mɔɔ n m ga m, m a kʊsɔrɔ waa a gannɔ ʊ y, a batɛm karɛ awɔɔ ʊ *Sɛnt-Ɛspri kan sɛ kɩ nɔ ʊ.
A y'a gʊr m a wɔ ʊ, a y'a tʊyaa waarɛ an a hɔyaa gʊr an a zar sɩ ʊ, bɩ, a sɛ k'a bɩr lihim bɩ nyam puur b'ʊ.» Yeezuu batɛm si A m bɩ, Yeezuu n a to Galilee ʊ, an bʊr Zʊrdɛn ʊ Zaan zi, k'a bɩ batɛm si.
Bɩ, Zaan a gu an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ y'ɩ ga m, ɩ batɛm ka mɔɔ ʊ, b'ɩbɩɩ ɩ ya, ɩ nɩ zɛm mɔɔ zi!» Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «A to k'an yɩ ncɩnaaʊ naa ma, bala, naa n'a karɛ, wɔɔ n hɔ haay k'an tɩrga Woso ma bɩ ba.» Zaan n yɛ kɛɛrɛ.
Yeezuu k'a batɛm bɩ si bɩ, a bɔ hi b'ʊ.
A m bɩ, brama n a gʊ, an Woso Ɛspri yɩ an nɩ zerle, am cirpʊʊrɛ hɔ bɩ m an bɩ zer la.
Leer n a to mim ʊ an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «Ba mɔɔ Nyɩ m na.
Mɔɔ ŋʊa gʊta.
A mɔɔ nɔ yɩ paan….»
